The author describes the history and organization of the CPT coding system. He then reviews the Evaluation and Management codes most frequently used for mental health services and discusses how to select the correct code based on level of service and/or time. Next he covers the specific CPT psychiatry codes, the circumstances under which each is most frequently used, and the reimbursement status of each. A discussion of documentation requirements and some recommendations for improved documentation are given. The article concludes with a review of current hot topics and future issues concerning coding and documentation.
