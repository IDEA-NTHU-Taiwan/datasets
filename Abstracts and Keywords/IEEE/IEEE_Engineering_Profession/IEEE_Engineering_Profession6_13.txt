Due to the uncertainty of emerging technologies development and their commercial prospect, it has important significant for technology investment decision-making on how to judge the prospect of emerging technology commercialization. Through the introduction on the relationship of bibliometrics and industry development, at first, this paper illuminates that it is a good method to research the emerging technology commercial prospect by using bibliometrics analysis methodology; then, the degree of emerging technology approaching to commercialization is illustrated by judging the technology maturity, at last, the degree of emerging technology approaching to commercialization success is analysis by judging whether the environment has being ready based on bibliometrics analysis approach.
