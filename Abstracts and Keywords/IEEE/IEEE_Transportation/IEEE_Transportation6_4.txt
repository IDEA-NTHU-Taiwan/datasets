At present, there are some academic studies on manufacture enterprise virtual operating, but a little academic study on service enterprise virtual operating. Moreover, the study focused on strategic performance appraisal system is almost blank and the empirical study on service enterprises has no discriminative samples. In this thesis, by using value chain analysis as the qualitative investigation angle, the author establishes performance appraisal indicator system of labor-intensive service enterprises virtual operating; by using fuzzy mathematics evaluation as quantitative research tool and a practical model analysis, probing into the executive way for labor-intensive service enterprises virtual operating strategic performance appraisals system, which offers theoretical viewpoint guide and practical model selection for labor-intensive service enterprises virtual operating strategic performance appraisal.
