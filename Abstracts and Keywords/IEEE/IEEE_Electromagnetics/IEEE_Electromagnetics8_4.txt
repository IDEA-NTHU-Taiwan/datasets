ACE, the Antenna Centre of Excellence, is a Network of Excellence funded by the European Commission. Its purpose is to restructure the scattered European antenna research. Starting 1 January 2004, it has a duration of 4 years and 51 participants from 17 European countries. 323 researchers and 130 PhD students are involved.
