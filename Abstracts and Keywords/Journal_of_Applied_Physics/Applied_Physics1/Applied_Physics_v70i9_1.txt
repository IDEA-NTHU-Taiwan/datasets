We have investigated the properties of Zn diffusion in the In0.53Ga0.47As/In0.52Al0.48As superlattice and have made optical guides delineated by Zninduced layer disordering in the multilayers. Measurements on the singlemode waveguides show loses as low as 2.3 dB/cm including coupling loss.
