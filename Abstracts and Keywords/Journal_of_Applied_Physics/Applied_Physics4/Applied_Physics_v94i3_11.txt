Persistent hole burning is observed in a europium doped sodium borate glass from 10 to 100 K. Hole burning efficiency increased 18× when 5% yttrium oxide was added to the glass mixture. Hole burning was observed up to 300 K in Eu3+, Y3+ Co-doped glass. © 2003 American Institute of Physics.
